WELCOME TO LINE THAI RESTAURANT
Our goal is to serve you authentic Thai food at its very best. We prepare each dish individually to ensure freshness and quality.
Our food can be VERY spicy.At their mildest, they are about as hot as Mexican food is served in the Southwest. At their spiciest, they are Thai HOT. But do not worry!
We will season each individual dish to your taste. We also have variety of non-spicy dishes for those who cannot handle the heat. To maintain their original taste,
therefore, some dishes may not be substituted or added. Also, there is no MSG added.
